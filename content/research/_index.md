## Research Interests

My research focuses on the development and application of computational intelligence methods, particularly at the intersection of evolutionary computation, deep learning, and optimization theory. Current research areas include:

**Multi-Objective Combinatorial Optimization**: Development of meta-learning algorithms leveraging evolutionary principles for complex optimization problems with multiple conflicting objectives.

**Deep Generative Models for Optimization**: Integration of generative adversarial networks (GANs) with evolutionary algorithms to enhance population diversity and solution quality in combinatorial optimization.

**Game-Theoretic Optimization**: Application of zero-sum game theory concepts to discrete optimization problems, developing novel algorithmic frameworks.

**Bioinformatics and Computational Biology**: Computational methods for biomarker identification, pathway analysis, and understanding disease progression mechanisms.

**Supply Chain Optimization**: Application of AI and optimization techniques to real-world supply chain challenges, including demand forecasting, inventory management, and logistics optimization.
