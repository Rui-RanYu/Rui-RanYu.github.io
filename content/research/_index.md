## 🎯 Research Interests

<div style="padding: 1.5rem; background: linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(102, 126, 234, 0.05) 0%, rgba(118, 75, 162, 0.05) 100%); border-radius: 15px; border-left: 4px solid #667eea;">

My research focuses on the intersection of evolutionary computation, deep learning, and optimization, with applications in:

- **🎲 Multi-Objective Combinatorial Optimization**: Developing meta-learning algorithms that leverage evolutionary principles
- **🎨 Deep Generative Models for Optimization**: Integrating GANs with evolutionary algorithms to enhance solution diversity
- **♟️ Game-Theoretic Optimization**: Applying zero-sum game theory concepts to discrete optimization problems
- **🧬 Bioinformatics**: Computational methods for biomarker identification and pathway analysis
